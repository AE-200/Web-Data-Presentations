Maximum Temperature



As expected, the temperature becomes increasingly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degress lattitude). More interestingly, however is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time the data was gathered.
